The story of the Black Spirit

A massive meteor crashed on the planet in prehistoric times. This older ancient meteor took a much more dramatic toll on the planet’s ecosystem: It introduced the black stones. In fact, those black stones actually formed The Great Valencian Black Desert. In a striking similarity to the Guild Wars franchise’s Crystal Desert in which the grains of sand are actually microscopic crystals, the Black Desert’s “sand” is primarily composed of microscopic black stones. And that’s where the Black Desert – and the game itself – gets its name.
